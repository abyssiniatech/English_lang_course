The Duolingo English Test (DET) is an online, adaptive English language proficiency test developed by Duolingo. It assesses a test-taker's English skills through a combination of multiple-choice questions, writing prompts, and a speaking sample. The test is designed to be convenient and accessible, allowing test-takers to take the test anytime, anywhere. It is used by thousands of universities for admissions purposes and is also accepted for student visas in some countries
